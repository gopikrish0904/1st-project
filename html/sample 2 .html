MAHATMA GANDHI

Alternate tittles:mohandas karamchand gandhi

(born october2,1869, porbandar,india-died january 30,1948,delhi)

Indian lawyer,politician ,social activitist,and writter who became the leader of the nationalist movement against the British rule of India.

As such ,he came to be considered the father of his country. gandhi is internationally esteemed for his doctrine of nonviolent protest to acheive political and social progress. ....

Youth

Gandhi was the youngest child of his father's fourth wife. His father-karamchand gandhi, who was the dewan of porbandar ,the capital of a small principality in western india under british suzerainty.

Years in South Africa

Africa was to present to gandhi challenges and oppurtunities that he hardley that he could hardly have conceived.In the end he would spend more than two decades there, returning to india only briefly in 1896-97. The youngest two of his four children were borm there.
